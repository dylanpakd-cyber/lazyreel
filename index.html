Real TikTok & Instagram videos from the past 6 months, decoded by our own pipeline. The more recent the upload, the stronger the signal, so the last 30 days are weighted highest. The number grows every day.

We validate, not vibe: the breakout laws are tested blind on concept-matched and same-creator pairs (83% within-creator, the cleanest tier) and we judge craft by reach over the creator's own baseline, not raw views. The full method is open: docs/methodology.
